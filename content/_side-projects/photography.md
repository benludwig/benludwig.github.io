Sometimes sitting for hours at a cafe just listening to the sounds of the city &mdash; talking about what to have for dinner even though we had just finished lunch.</p>

  <div class="skinny">
    <h2>Lithuania</h2>
    <p>There are two distinct cultures in Lithuania: Lithuanians and Russians living in Lithuania. Both groups are incredibly passionate about the local basketball team. During my visit, I also encountered a restaurant only referred to as, "Old Lady's House." Which was, as you would guess, an elderly woman who served lunch in her dining room every afternoon. Open to the public and cash only.</p>

  <div class="skinny">
    <h2>Isla Mujeres</h2>
    <p>It translates to "Island of Beautiful Women" and my wife was with me so I happen to be in agreement that this is accurate. After a friend's wedding, we took a day trip to swim in the ocean, have several beers and of course, to drive a golf cart around the island &mdash; though not necessarily in that order. Seedy does not begin to describe the cash transaction required to rent one of these golf carts &mdash; especially when someone asked for my I.D. and then tried to steal my license &mdash; but it's was well worth it.</p>
